While You Wait

3 Things You Can Do Right Now

This helps our plumber arrive prepared and limits any further damage.

🚨 If You Have an Emergency
1
Find and turn off your main water stopcock
This limits water damage while we are on the way. It is usually under the kitchen sink or where the water pipe enters your home.
2
Take photos of the problem
Photos help our plumber arrive prepared with the right parts, which means we fix it faster in a single visit.
3
Move valuables away from the affected area
Protect anything important from potential water damage while we are on the way to you.
📋 What to Have Ready for Our Visit
✓
Know where your main water stopcock is
Our plumber may need to turn this off at the start of the job.
✓
Note when the problem started
And whether anything changed recently — new appliance, recent work, unusual sounds.
✓
Clear access to the affected area
Move anything blocking access so we can get to work immediately on arrival.
✓
Have your preferred payment method ready
We accept cash, card and bank transfer. Payment is after the job — when you are happy.
